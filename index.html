ProofLayer

AI ProofLayer is a control layer that decides whether an AI output is allowed to proceed, based on whether it meets explicit, measurable outcome guarantees — and records verifiable evidence of that decision.

The Problem

AI systems make claims — grounded, safe, compliant — but there’s no standard way to prove they actually meet those claims in production. ProofLayer turns promises into verifiable evidence.

How It Works

  • Evaluate an AI output against an explicit outcome claim.
  • Decide ALLOW or BLOCK based on measurable thresholds.
  • Generate audit-ready evidence artifacts for compliance and review.

What ProofLayer Produces

Each decision produces:

  • Deterministic scores
  • Immutable Proof IDs
  • PDF & JSON evidence packs
